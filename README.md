### 🚀 Featured Projects

I believe in "showing," not just "telling." Here are a few projects where I've turned raw data into actionable insights:

#### 1. Indian Flight Booking & Pricing Analysis
*A deep dive into airline strategy.*
* **The Problem:** Using a dataset of 300,000+ records to uncover pricing volatility.
* **The Solution:** Engineered multi-table JOINs and NTILE window functions to map how last-minute pricing shifts for different classes.
* **Key Insight:** Calculated a 600% premium gap for business class on the Delhi-Mumbai corridor.
* [View on GitHub](https://github.com/oyenugatoluwalase3-sudo/Flight-Booking-SQL-Analysis)

#### 2. Nigerian Healthcare EDA
*Visualizing the state of our infrastructure.*
* **The Problem:** Analyzing healthcare accessibility and disease burden across Nigeria.
* **The Solution:** Used Python (Pandas/Plotly) to create interactive visualizations of facility data across 36 states.
* **Key Insight:** Tracked trends from 2015-2023 to highlight geographic hotspots where healthcare support is most needed.
* [View on GitHub](https://github.com/oyenugatoluwalase3-sudo/nigerian-healthcare-eda)

#### 3. Chocolate Sales Dashboard
*Turning sales data into revenue strategy.*
* **The Problem:** Understanding global sales performance and regional drivers.
* **The Solution:** An end-to-end Power BI report tracking 6 countries and 25 salespersons.
* **Key Insight:** Identified that Australia was the top-performing market at $2.4M and that 2023 performance consistently outpaced the previous year.
* [View on GitHub](https://github.com/oyenugatoluwalase3-sudo/chocolate-sales-dashboard)
