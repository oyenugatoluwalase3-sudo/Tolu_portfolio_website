About Me

The analyst behind the numbers.

My name is Oyenuga Toluwalase Elijah — a 300-level Computer Science student at the Federal University of Technology Akure (FUTA), also enrolled in Applied Data Science at WorldQuant University, finishing in 2025.

I didn't plan to get into data. A leader at my church pointed me away from no-code tools toward something with real depth. We started with Python. Then data science. Then it clicked — I love the part where a messy spreadsheet becomes a clean, honest story. I especially love dashboards and SQL.

“The process might be slow — but I believe I'll eventually get there.”

Still building, still learning, still making milestones one at a time. I use AI heavily — not as a shortcut, but to learn faster and go deeper than a textbook ever let me.

Outside the screen: fast typist, Clash of Clans player (morning and night), and someone who happily spends 9–12 hours in a library thinking more than reading.
